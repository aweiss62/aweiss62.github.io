    The Aston - Investigation, Accountability: First-year students who live in the Aston oftentimes have bad experiences, from cold shower water to Internet outages, even no heat. As the person in charge of SBA special Ad-Hoc Policy Committee, I have already begun an investigation into the Aston. What this investigation has demonstrated thus far is that the Aston is a complex situation due to the number of departments involved in its operation not to mention the number of interests. However, the continued student complaints about the facility demonstrate that something needs to be done to maintain the habitability of dorm rooms.

    With my five years of experience working for the Department of Housing at Georgia Tech, four year with RHA, and broad understanding of greater university dealings, I want to untangle this mess and present the Administration with a cohesive way of solving this major problem; preferably before we end up on AboveTheLaw. Students, especially first-years, have enough on their mind without worrying about whether there will be a leak in their room when they return home or whether they will have Internet to work on their assignments.

    Library Second Floor Entrance - For those students who study on the second floor of the library, you are likely aware that the second floor entrance closes very early compared to the downstairs entrance. This makes it difficult to access amenities such as the bathroom right outside of those doors. As there is only one bathroom in the library in the basement, this pretty much blows. I hope to talk with facilities about opening this entrance for longer hours for the convenience of those who study on the second floor.
