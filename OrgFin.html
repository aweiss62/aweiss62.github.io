Streamline Finance Submission


Summary:

  • Currently: Orgs must submit numerous docs through four mediums
  • Solution: One stop shop on website
    • ---------------------------------------------

    I want to streamline the financial submission process. As a 1L I wrote the SBA Finance Policy to aid organizations. Prior to that, financial regulations and the rubrics for judging allocations were spread out over five different mediums (emails, SBA bylaws, SBA constitution, treasurer training .ppt, and the ad-hoc submission page). The Finance Policy consolidated that into one location, to create a kind of handbook for treasurers to go to. Now I want to take on the reimbursement and submission processes.

    Itâ€™s ridiculous that orgs have to submit the same information through four mediums to three different people to get money (Ad-Hoc, Audit, VP of Finance)! There should be a one-stop shop where an org would input the data once before clicking 'send.' With my experience in coding, along with the knowledge I gained when I wrote the SBA Finance Policy, I know this can easily be done and will not rest until it is.
