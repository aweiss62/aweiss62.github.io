Major Goals

2016-2017

  • Alcohol Policy - HPPS

    Self-Governance & Graduate Alcohol Policy - Law Students like to blow off steam, accountability for which should remain within the law school. It is imperative to create a new AND workable system.

  • Grade Publication

    As EVP, I formed a Committee to investigate the timelines and methods of our peer institutions. With this data, as President, I would work with the administration to actively enforce the deadlines already established.

  • Small Firm Career Fair

    OCI and PSRP are great programs to connect students with job opportunities in large firms and the public sector respectively. As President, I would work with the Career Center create a Small Firm Career Fair.

  • Financial Streamlining for Orgs

    I want to streamline the financial submission process for reimbursements. With the knowledge I gained when I wrote the Finance Policy to make a one-stop-shop submission page on the website; I know this can be done and would rest until then.
