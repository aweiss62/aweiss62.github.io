Bar Review Input


Summary:

  • Bar Review Suggestions - Creat a button on the website to solicit recommendations
    • ---------------------------------------------

    Maintaining the Awesomeness: The programming committee this year has worked tremendously hard to find great bars to accommodate our humongous class sizes nearly every Thursday evening of the semester and we’ve taken note of the bars that did not (we no longer go there). The committee has also secured great deals for those who are able to attend those events.

    As President, I hope to keep this momentum going, while simultaneously taking student feedback into account. For this reason I would launch a student Bar Brainstorm Button on the SBA Website. With my programming (get it?) background I know it can be done easily and this way the Programming Committee will have an endless supply of venues to choose from. Furthermore, I will continue to maintain the university’s alcohol policy to avoid the potential of losing all alcohol events, which is something that happened three years ago. I am very familiar with the policy based on my experience and I will ensure that the policy is followed so that we can preserve Bar Reviews and other social events year round.
